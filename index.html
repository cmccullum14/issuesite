EduCare: The Place That Educates and Cares

It’s known that when students have fewer resources outside of the school building, they struggle inside the classroom as well. Students are being deprived of the opportunity to pursue their goals and career ambitions due to the lack of quality education and materials. That’s where EduCare can step in to help. EduCare can provide these necessary materials to help low-income students take the first step towards a successful future.
